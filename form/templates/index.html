It’s Time to #FixDialysis

Dialysis helps people with kidney failure stay alive, but patient care is suffering from inadequate staffing and unsanitary conditions. That’s because the dialysis companies value profit more than their patients and caregivers like us. To companies like DaVita and Fresenius, we are all just numbers on their balance sheets. But we are More than Numbers, and we can #FixDialysis by improving staffing and conditions in our facilities.

We can start by improving staffing levels so that RNs, PCTs, and Social Workers like us are better able to safely and effectively care for patients. Using this tool, you can take a look at what your personal, daily staffing looks like and compare it to safer levels. Get started below.
